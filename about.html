About SmartCampus

Your Digital College Management Platform

What is SmartCampus?

SmartCampus is an advanced College Management System designed to simplify, automate and digitalize the daily operations of colleges. It provides a centralized platform for managing students, teachers, notices, events, study materials, complaints, attendance, and communication.

Key Features

  • Admin, Teacher & Student Dashboards
  • Digital Notice Board & Event Management
  • Study Material Upload & Access
  • Complaint & Feedback System
  • Test Creation & Submission Module
  • Performance Tracking & Reports

About VP Institute of Management Studies & Research

VP Institute of Management Studies & Research, Sangli, is committed to delivering high-quality education and fostering innovation. The SmartCampus system is designed to support the institute in achieving a fully digital, transparent, and student-friendly environment.
